Instructions

Decide how much inventory you would like to buy, you can buy more each turn.

Each game the profitability of your item will change, see how well your sales you do and adapt to your conditions

The goal of the game is to become a profitable company by the twelfth turn, aka you need over 10,000 units by then

Each Turn you incur monthly recurring costs to keep your business going, thankfully your business is online, so this are much lower than brick and mortar
